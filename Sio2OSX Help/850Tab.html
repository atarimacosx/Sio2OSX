This tab allows the user to chose what devices are connected to each of the 4 emulated 850 Serial ports.  By default, port 1 is connected to the Internet modem simulation, and ports 2-4 are not connected.  Note, the internet modem simulation and each physical serial port on the Macintosh may only be connected to one emulated serial port at a time.  Also note, that the physical serial port on the Macintosh that is used for the main SIO connection to the Atari is not available for use as a 850 connection, and will be grayed out in the menus.
